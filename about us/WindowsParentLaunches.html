About Us

Hello, and thank you for stopping by :)

I’m a mother of two, passionate about parenting, education and technology. Previously known as Windows Phone Parent, Windows Parent is the one and only site dedicated to reviewing and promoting family-friendly Windows 8 and Windows Phone applications from a parent perspective.

My mission for the Windows Parent is:

1) To provide parents with an opportunity to discover Windows 8 and Windows Phone applications that enhance their lives or educate as well as entertain their children.

2) To be an active participant of the Windows and Windows Phone ecosystem, by voicing the needs of parents and children to the developer community in order to encourage the development of high quality family-friendly apps.

3) To support the developer community by offering promotional opportunities within our platform so they can reach thousands of Windows Parents in English and Spanish speaking markets.

Please don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions, suggestions or comments.

Cheers!
