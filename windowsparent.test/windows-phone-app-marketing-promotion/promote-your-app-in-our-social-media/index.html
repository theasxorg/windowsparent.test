Promote your app on our social media

What type of promotions can you do on our social media?

Leverage our social media channels to announce anything related to your family-friendly Windows Phone or Windows 8 apps. Announce app launches, updates, improvements, bug-fixes, price changes, awards, an anything that you would like to share with our 4,500 followers. You app doesn’t have to be in our catalog in order for us to promote it on our social media, it only has to be family-friendly.

Why promote your app to on our Spanish social media?

Windows Phone supports 17 Spanish-speaking markets. There has also been a huge gain in Spanish markets such Mexico where Windows Phone currently holds over 25% of the smart phone market share. We have a strong Spanish-speaking social media presence, so why not take advantage of it?

... but first the fine print

We are committed to promoting quality family-friendly apps. We reserve the right to not promote your app in our social media channels if it doesn't meet our standards. After requesting a promotion, the Windows Parent team will evaluate if it is a good fit for our platform and if it meets our family-friendly standards. If your app doesn't meet our standards, we won't promote it in our social media and, we will fully refund your money.
